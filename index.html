What to expect

Clear steps, no guesswork — here’s what a standard visit looks like.

Step 1

We inspect properly

We check entry points, moisture zones, nesting areas, and confirm the pest + severity.

  • Identify source & contributing factors
  • Explain what we find (plain English)
  • Safety considerations (kids/pets)
Step 2

Targeted treatment

We use a pest-specific method — not a one-size spray. Treatment depends on environment and risk.

  • Crack/crevice & hotspot focus
  • Internal + external as required
  • Clean, tidy application
Step 3

Prevention plan

You leave with practical next steps to stop the problem returning — sealing, hygiene, monitoring.

  • Simple prevention checklist
  • Common causes to fix
  • Follow-up guidance if needed
